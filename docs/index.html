Main Gate of the Chinese Garden

I slept, and drifted into dreams,

Where ponds reflected bamboo streams.

Inspired by the Craft of Gardens,

Each stone and gate wove gentle themes.

I woke to find myself in a serene Chinese garden. The morning mist hugged the curved roofs, lanterns swayed gently, and the air smelled of jasmine. A feeling of calm and wonder filled me, as if the book I read guided me here.
