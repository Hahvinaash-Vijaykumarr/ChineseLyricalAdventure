Chinese Garden Inspiration

The iconic Cloud Pagoda in Singapore’s Chinese Garden, a seven-storey tower inspired by ancient temples like Nanjing’s Linggu Temple, dominates the skyline. Its layered roofs and surrounding zodiac sculptures make it both a cultural landmark and a viewpoint offering panoramic vistas of the gardens and lake below

My Dream Garden

In my dream garden, the pagoda is redesigned to be smaller and painted red, providing an intimate landmark while keeping the layered roof and surrounding gardens that honor the original Cloud Pagoda’s spirit.

Rising tier, red roofs in the sun,
Steps echo where quiet souls run.
Cloud Pagoda stands so tall,
Shadows play along the wall.
